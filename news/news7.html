Student Yara Allam Wins First Place in University Chess Championship

As part of the activities of the Chess Championship organized by the Social Activities and Trips Department at the General Administration of Youth Welfare, student Yara Allam Ibrahim – first-year – won first place at the university level (Women’s Singles), as well as first place at the university level (Blitz).

The championship was held from 6–12 November 2022 at the Student Activities Complex of the General Administration of Youth Welfare.
The Faculty Administration extends its sincere wishes to its students for continued success and progress.

Attendance

  • Attendance:

    • A student is not allowed to sit for the final exam unless they have attended at least 75% of the practical teaching hours of the course.

  • Absence from the Final Exam:
    1. If a student is absent from the final exam of any course and submits a compelling excuse accepted by the College's Student Affairs Committee within no more than two days from the exam date, and completes an Incomplete Course Request Form, they will be given the temporary grade "Incomplete (I)" for that course, provided that they have obtained at least 60% of the coursework marks and have not been prohibited from taking the final exam (see Article 16: Attendance).
    2. In the case of an "Incomplete (I)" grade, the student will be allowed to take the final exam during the first week of the following semester or as determined by the College Council. The student’s total grade in that course will be the sum of the final exam grade plus the coursework marks previously obtained during the semester.
